Number 1 destination stop

New Zealand is an island country in the southwestern Pacific Ocean.

  • Location: A beautiful island country in the Pacific Ocean.
  • Major cities: Auckland, Wellington (capital), Christchurch.
  • Official languages: English and Māori.

Why Visit New Zealand

  • Stunning landscapes (mountains, beaches, lakes).
  • Adventure activities (bungee jumping, skydiving, hiking).
  • Rich Māori culture and traditions.

Things to do while there

  • Go bungee jumping in Queenstown.
  • Visit glowworm caves in Waitomo.
  • Explore the beaches and waterfalls of the Bay of Islands.
  • Try adventure sports like skiing and white-water rafting.

Top Attractions

  • Milford Sound- A breathtaking fjord with waterfalls and cruises.
  • Hobbiton – A must-visit for "Lord of the Rings" fans.
  • Mount Cook – The highest mountain in New Zealand, great for hiking.
  • Rotorua – Famous for geothermal activity and Māori cultural experiences.
