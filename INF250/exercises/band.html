BTS

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; Bangtan Sonyeondan; Bulletproof Boy Scouts), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010.

It consists of seven members namely:

  • Kim Nam Joon - Leader
  • Kim Seok Jin
  • Min Yoon Gi
  • Jung Ho Seok
  • Park Ji Min
  • Kim Tae Hyung
  • Jeong Jung Kook
